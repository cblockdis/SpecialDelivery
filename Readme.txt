Player controls
  Run: Left or Right
  Jump: Space
  Enter Door: Up
  
Ship Controls
  Move: L, R, U, D
  Select: Enter (when on a circle)
  Cancel / Leave: Esc
  
To leave player ship just walk off the screen to the left. If you haven't entered 
the navigation room and selected a 'circle' it will not let you leave and will just 
put you back in the player ship until you choose a location.
